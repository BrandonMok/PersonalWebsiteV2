Favorite Place - Individual

Based on the Web & Mobile II's course, the project required the creation an informative website about a favorite place or hometown. Originating from a small town that's 1 mile in size, I didn't have a lot of interesting information to provide; therefore, I chose a favorite place.

Most people would think of more popular places such as New York City, Hawaii, or DisneyWorld, but I chose HersheyPark. It's not necessarily my favorite place, but it's different from the most widely picked areas. I love chocolate and candy anyways, so I got to provide information about an amusement park focused on these two items: sweet right?!

The project was relatively large as it consisted of two major parts. The first part was creating the project and content while the second was during the last half of the semester where we were to include different technologies learned during the course. My project consists of 14+ pages, a javascript slideshow, PHP form data collection and verification, a connection to PHPMyAdmin, and a comments section.
