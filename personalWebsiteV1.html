Personal Website V1

My first personal website was created over the summer of 2018 as I spent numerous hours designing and creating the pages to my liking. This project, although not a lot, provided an extreme sense of pride as I felt that it was a representation of my work to show to others. I could finally redirect people to my site if they wanted ideas for projects, but I have also used it for potential employers to gain a perspective and a way to contact me.

I further created this project as I wanted a website that was mine. I've made multiple individual websites in the past, but those were course specific to display assignments and projects. Having my own website meant more expression and individualization as I can display my style and provide an idea of the person I am.

Overtime, however, I felt that the project served its purpose, but was not quite what I wanted. As a result, I wanted to re-design and make the site more intuitive and professional than that of the original which is the product of the current site you are on.
