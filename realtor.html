Realtor Website - Group

Based on the Web and Mobile II course, groups of 4 members, including myself, were given the task to create a project based on an informative topic.


My group didn't have a specific idea, but one member mentioned that his mom, a home realtor, was wanting a personal business website. With no previous ideas in mind, we all agreed as we further thought that it would be an interesting experience.


I acted as the team leader as I held meetings, communicated daily with updates and requirements, and distributed tasks. I made sure that each task would be completed by a certain date as I checked-up on everyone and provided help along the way.


Besides watching over the group, I also made several pages and layouts. I further consolidated the code so that it was a fully functional product.


We faced a large problem during the project, however, as one group member's attitude changed towards the end of the project. They didn't fulfill their responsibities like they stated, and waited until the last few hours until the due date. The resulting solution was to quickly communicate with the member while they worked on their part as we were able to finish the project before the deadline, but the quality and functionality wasn't what I expected or desired.

  3. JS
  4. PHP
