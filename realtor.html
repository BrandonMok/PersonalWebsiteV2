Realtor Website - Group

Based on the Web and Mobile II course, groups of 4 members were given the task to create a project based on an informative topic.

My group didn't have a specific idea, but one member mentioned that his mother, a home realtor, was wanting a personal business website. With no previous ideas, we all agreed on the idea as we further thought that it would be an interesting experience.

I acted as team leader as I held meetings, communicated daily with updates and requirements, and distributed tasks. I made sure that each task would be completed by a certain date as I checked-up on everyone.

Besides watching over the group, I also made several pages and layouts to contribute to the progress of the project. I further combined everyone's code so that it was a fully functional product.

We faced a big problem during the project, however, as one group member's attitude changed towards the end of the project. They didn't fulfill their responsibities like they stated, and waited until the last few hours until the due date to fulfill their part. The resulting solution was to quickly communicate with the member while they worked: not the best solution, but was successful while other previous actions and solutions failed.

As a result of the obstacle, the appearance of the project failed to meet our expectations as the lacking member was responsible for the header, footer, database, and parts of CSS. That being said, previous versions before the member's part, appeared well and professional.
