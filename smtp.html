SMTP - Group

From the Computational Problem Solving Info Domain II course, the final project was to create a mail client and server that followed the SMTP protocol in Java to communicate with other groups' programs.


Each group was responsible for following the protocol and to have the ability to connect and utilize eachother's mail servers to send and receive messages.


I was responsible for helping create the SMTP protocol, storing user's information such as username and IP, and encrypting the messages sent between all users and groups.


The class had several problems during the testing phase, but my group was successful at communicating with three of the total six. The success and failure to connect to all six was primarily due to groups not following the protocol correctly, technical issues, and other design issues.
